Welcome To METS

Maritime Education and Training Services Pte Ltd (METS) is a company incorporated in Singapore with a management team of Singaporean maritime education and training (MET) professionals who were lecturers of the Maritime and Port Authority of Singapore (MPA) and the private training services industry. They were also ex-master mariners who bring with them a total of more than 50 years of seafaring and marine industry-related experience to METS.


METS is therefore confident that it has a well-qualified, experienced and highly motivated management team to serve and exceed the expectations of its customers.
