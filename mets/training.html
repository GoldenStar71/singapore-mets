Power boating activities are going to increase in a big way in Singapore especially with the establishment of the Marina Reservoir and catalyzed by the activities that would be brought about by the Marina Bay Sands, Resort World Sentosa and mega marina projects at Sentosa and Keppel Bay in addition to existing marina all over Singapore. Also boosted by the gradual freeing up of water catchment areas e.g. reservoirs, for recreational activities, volume of power boating activities will soar even further.

In Singapore, one needs to hold a PPCDL if he wants to drive a powered pleasure craft within the port waters. To obtain a PPCDL, he needs to complete a mandatory course that is required by the Maritime and Port Authority of Singapore (MPA). This is the PPCDL Course. METS is the training service provider to run PPCDL Courses for the Marina Country Club which is approved by the MPA to run their courses at the Marina Country Club at Ponggol). More information on the application and renewal of the licence can be found in a procedure document titled Guide to Application / Renewal of a Powered Pleasure Craft Driving Licence in MPA website.

Interested parties for PPCDL Courses, please enroll with METS. Interested parties may also submit an online registration. Registrants should also have the medical report form completed by a qualified medical practitioner (GP) before enrolling for the course. Medical check can also be done after the course. Organisations or Groups requesting for a specialised course (for a group of at least 6 participants), to suit their requirements or schedules can write to enquiry@mets1.sg .
